My Story

Why I Code
I jumped into Computer Science because I love figuring out how things work. There's something amazing about taking an idea and turning it into something real with code.
Growing Up Tech-Curious
Ever since I got my hands on my first computer, I was hooked. I was that kid who would take apart phones and try to put them back together (sometimes successfully!). In 2017, I took on my biggest challenge yet building my own computer from scratch. After weeks of research and saving up, I finally got all the parts and dove in. The moment it first powered on was pure magic - and that's when I knew tech was my future.
From Tinkerer to Developer
My coding adventure kicked off in high school with Python. It was like discovering a superpower, suddenly I could create actual programs! These days, I work with all sorts of languages and tools, including:
  • Building websites that look great and work smoothly
  • Creating apps with Python and Java
  • Working with databases to make apps smarter
  • Using Git to keep track of all my code
  • Working on projects with other developers
